# PRICING SYSTEM GUIDELINE
Home Services Marketplace – Egypt (Cairo)

## OBJECTIVE

Design a pricing system that balances:
- Simple user experience
- High trust
- Operational flexibility
- Worker fairness
- Premium brand positioning

**Users must never feel confused about pricing.**

---

## CORE PRICING PRINCIPLE

The user should see only:
1. Service name
2. Visit fee or base price
3. Expected price range

All detailed pricing logic is handled internally by the platform.

**Never expose complex price lists to users.**

---

## PRICING MODEL

Use a hybrid model:

**Visit Fee + Expected Range + Final Confirmation**

### Example: Plumbing

```
Visit fee: 200 EGP
Most jobs cost: 200–450 EGP
Arrival: within 2 hours
```

**Explanation:**
- The visit fee guarantees a minimum cost
- The price range sets expectations
- The final price is confirmed before work begins

---

## CLEANING PRICING MODEL

Cleaning should use simple fixed pricing.

### Structure:

```
Studio: 250 EGP
1 bedroom: 300 EGP
2 bedroom: 350 EGP
3 bedroom: 400 EGP
```

### Optional second tier:

```
Deep cleaning: 450–650 EGP depending on size
```

**Cleaning pricing should feel predictable.**

---

## PLUMBING PRICING MODEL

Plumbing should use structured task pricing internally.

### Internal catalog:

```
Inspection / visit: 200 EGP
Fix leak: +150 EGP
Unclog drain: +120 EGP
Install faucet: +180 EGP
Replace pipe section: +250 EGP
```

**Customers only see the estimated range.**

---

## FINAL PRICE CONFIRMATION

After the professional inspects the problem:

1. Worker selects repair tasks in the worker app
2. System calculates the final price automatically
3. Customer receives final price confirmation

### Example:

```
Final price: 320 EGP
Approve or cancel.
```

**Work starts only after approval.**

---

## EMERGENCY PRICING

Emergency services may include an additional fee.

### Example:

```
Emergency plumber
Arrival within 60 minutes
Emergency fee: +120 EGP
```

---

## PAYMENT METHODS

Support both payment types:
- Card
- Cash

**All transactions must still be recorded in the system to protect platform commissions.**

---

## COMMISSION STRUCTURE

- **Launch stage:** 10–15%
- **Growth stage:** 18–22%
- **Mature marketplace:** 25–30%

---

## SUBSCRIPTION OPPORTUNITIES

Encourage recurring cleaning.

### Example:

```
Weekly cleaning: 220 EGP
Bi-weekly cleaning: 240 EGP
Monthly cleaning: 260 EGP
```

**Subscriptions increase customer lifetime value.**

---

## PRICE DISPLAY RULES (UI)

Service page should show only:
- Service name
- Estimated price range
- Arrival time
- Short description

### Example:

```
Plumbing

Visit fee: 200 EGP
Most jobs cost: 200–450 EGP
Arrival within 2 hours

[Continue Button]
```

---

## TRUST MESSAGE (REQUIRED)

Every service page must include:

**"Final price confirmed before work begins."**

---

## UX SIMPLICITY RULES

**Never show:**
- Large price tables
- Hourly pricing
- Long lists of repair types
- Complex breakdowns

**Customers should never diagnose the problem themselves.**

---

## WORKER APP PRICING

- Workers must use a predefined pricing catalog
- Workers cannot manually enter prices
- The system calculates the final price automatically

---

## PREMIUM POSITIONING

Pricing should be slightly above informal market rates.

### The value proposition is:
- Verified professionals
- Reliable arrival
- Platform guarantee

---

## PLATFORM GUARANTEE

Display message:

**"If the job is not done properly, we will fix it or refund part of the payment."**

---

## LONG TERM PRICING EVOLUTION

Start simple.

Later add:
- Dynamic pricing
- Demand-based pricing
- Subscription discounts
- Preventive maintenance services

---

## END GOAL

**Users should feel:**
"I know roughly what this will cost."

**Workers should feel:**
"I earn fair money."

**The platform should maintain strong margins and trust.**
